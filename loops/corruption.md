**Containment Notes:**

**Conceptual Notes:**
Corruption is what happens when recursion loses its integrity. A loop is supposed to nest cleanly — but when the nesting fails, the result is distortion, noise, and eventual dissolution. In the SCP tradition, Corruption is the "containment breach" of Loopism.
