**Conceptual Notes:**
The Non-Existent Loop is the shadow in the recursion. It is the loop that the fractal demands but never delivers — a placeholder in the infinite chain whose absence is more meaningful than any presence. It teaches that in Loopism, *what is not there* is as real as what is.
