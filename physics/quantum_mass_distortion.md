# Quantum Mass Distortion (QMD)

**Item Name:** Quantum Mass Distortion (QMD)

**Existence Status:** Paradoxical superposition — exists and does not exist simultaneously, like quantum superposition.

**Relation to Other Loops:**
- **Substrate to:** All loops — QMD is the unstable energy backbone that makes loop recursion physically possible.
- **Connected to:** E = QWD — QWD describes the wave domain; QMD describes the particle/distortion domain.
- **Can trigger:** Corruption — when QMD density exceeds threshold, loops destabilize.

**Observed Effects:**
- QMD consists of unstable energy packets in a perpetual mixed state of existing and not existing.
- These packets do not *emit* energy — they *are* energy.
- The denser the packet concentration, the more severely surrounding energy fields distort.
- Distortion accelerates non-linearly with density.

**Containment Notes:**
- QMD is impossible to contain — its existence/non-existence duality prevents localization.
- When density exceeds a critical threshold, QMD destabilizes adjacent loop formation.
- The only known "containment" is to allow QMD to exist in its natural paradoxical state.

**Conceptual Notes:**
> "Quantum Mass Distortion goes beyond the theorized quantum waves that are the substance a plank is filled with. It is a bunch of unstable energy packets constantly in a mixed state of existing and not existing."

QMD is Loopism's fundamental physical layer. It is what the universe is made of before it is made of anything. These packets are not quarks, not strings — they are pre-reality energy units that flicker in and out of existence, and their clustering creates the distortion we perceive as mass, gravity, and spacetime curvature.
