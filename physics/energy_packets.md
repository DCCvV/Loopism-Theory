# Energy Packets

**Item Name:** Energy Packets

**Existence Status:** Quantum superposition — each packet exists and does not exist at the same time.

**Relation to Other Loops:**
- **Constituent of:** Quantum Mass Distortion — QMD is an aggregation of energy packets.
- **Component of:** Distortion Clusters — packets clump into clusters.
- **Scale reference to:** Quarks — packets are 10⁻²⁸⁶ times smaller than quarks.

**Observed Effects:**
- Each packet is a self-contained unit that behaves like a universe.
- Packets do not emit energy because they *are* energy — emission is a property of composite systems.
- A single packet cannot be isolated; attempting to measure it collapses its superposition into one state, losing the paradox.

**Containment Notes:**
- Individual packets cannot be contained because their existence is probabilistic.
- Any containment apparatus must itself be composed of packets, creating a self-referential paradox.
- Research protocol: study packets in clusters only; isolate none.

**Conceptual Notes:**
> "The packets aren't quarks — but they are 10⁻²⁸⁶ times smaller. The packets are like universes."

Energy packets are the smallest ontological units in Loopism physics. They are smaller than anything in standard particle physics by an astronomical factor. Each packet is a universe in miniature — a complete loop of existence and non-existence. This makes every physical object a multiverse of packets.
